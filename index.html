1. Introduction

The spread of terrorism throughout the world has seriously endangered the safety of human life and property. Affected by such factors as the protracted covid-19 pandemic, intensified global geopolitical confrontation, and continued global economic downturn, the development of international terrorism and extremism has become more complex, and the international counter-terrorism situation remains grim.

We hope that the project can provide researchers with analytical data support, can also be interested in the provision of corresponding query services to contribute to international counter-terrorism.

2. Dataset

Global Terrorism Database is an open-source data set for global terrorism events. It contains information of more than 180,000 terrorist attacks worldwide, from 1970 to 2017. The database is maintained by researchers at the National Consortium for the Study of Terrorism and Responses to Terrorism (START), headquartered at the University of Maryland. We want to use this database to build an analysis platform for users to gain information of global terrorism.
